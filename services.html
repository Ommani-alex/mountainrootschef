Private Chef Services

I'm Chef David Breeden of Mountain Roots Catering. With 13 years of experience in the culinary industry—ranging from country clubs and restaurants to casinos and allergy-conscious meal prep—I bring creative, custom dining experiences into your home or event space.

While my specialty is refined Appalachian cuisine, I craft everything from comforting classics to globally inspired fine dining. Every meal is tailored to your tastes, preferences, and dietary needs.

Why Choose a Personal Chef?

With a personal chef, you enjoy restaurant-level cuisine at home—without grocery shopping, crowded restaurants, or compromise. I provide seasonal, fresh ingredients and full-service dining that fits your preferences, allergies, and lifestyle.

Custom Baked Goods

From decadent chocolate bars to towering carrot cakes, I create custom small-batch baked goods for birthdays, holidays, and everyday indulgence. Options include gluten-free and allergy-friendly items by request.

Event Catering

I offer flexible catering for weddings, parties, and professional events. Choose from curated menus or work with me to build one from scratch. I can accommodate most dietary needs, serving groups of up to 50 guests depending on venue and service type.

Cooking Classes

Learn to cook like a chef in your own kitchen. I offer hands-on lessons for individuals, couples, and small groups—perfect for birthdays, date nights, or team building. Topics range from Appalachian cooking to knife skills and seasonal specialties.
