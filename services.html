Our Services

Mountain Roots Chef offers a range of culinary experiences crafted with heart, skill, and a deep appreciation for Appalachian heritage. Whether you're planning a small gathering or an intimate dinner at home, we bring flavor and hospitality to your table.

Chef David Breeden offers elevated at-home dining experiences. With 13 years of professional culinary experience, he delivers multi-course, restaurant-quality meals tailored to your tastes and dietary preferences.

  • Locally sourced ingredients
  • Personalized service in your home
  • Three to five course offerings

From decadent bars to seasonal cakes and everything in between, our small-batch baked goods are crafted to order and designed for celebration. Allergy-friendly options available.

Event Catering

Whether you're planning a wedding, party, or community gathering, we provide flexible and delicious catering options. Menus are built to suit your theme, budget, and guest list.

Cooking Classes

Learn to cook Appalachian classics or explore global cuisine in a relaxed, hands-on format. Perfect for couples, groups, or team building events.
