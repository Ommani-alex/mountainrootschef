Book Your Private Chef Experience

Chef David Breeden offers personalized dining experiences crafted to your taste and dietary preferences. Below is a breakdown of pricing tiers, meal structure, and what to expect when you book Mountain Roots Catering.

Meal Structure

  • Starter Course: Appetizer, soup, or salad
  • Main Course: Protein + two sides
  • Final Course: Dessert or finger foods

Pricing Tiers

$55 per person

Mid-grade restaurant quality—chicken, pork, beef, and some seafood.

$65–$75 per person

$85+ per person

Fine dining with premium proteins like lobster, scallops, and filet mignon.

An $80 non-refundable retainer is required to secure your date. The final invoice will be sent 7 days before your event. A $50 travel fee applies to all bookings located more than 40 miles outside of Haywood County.
