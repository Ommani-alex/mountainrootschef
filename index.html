Welcome

Welcome to Mountain Roots. I'm David Breeden, a personal chef and baker rooted in the mountains of Western North Carolina. With over 13 years of experience, I craft refined Appalachian cuisine and fresh, small-batch baked goods inspired by heritage and heart. From cozy, chef-prepared dinners to crave-worthy treats like handmade carrot cakes and chocolate bars, everything I make is personalized and thoughtfully sourced. Let me help you turn any occasion into something unforgettable.
